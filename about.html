    Who We Are

    Edu Bid is an online webapp that is designed to be an auction system to help students access resources and products at a ceap and affordable price. We were established in 2023 with the desire of making a market for students from disadvantaged backgrounds and cannot afford to purchase products from the shop. With this platform we aim to unite students because we understand the financial frustration faced by students. However, EduBid is bridging the gap to excellence.
